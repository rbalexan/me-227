\secGR{Understeer Gradient}

To give our simulation results later in the problem a little more context, let's find Niki's understeer gradient.

Follow the prompts in \GRno{} to create a script to calculate Niki's understeer gradient. Find the understeer gradient
in units of \si{\radian/\m/\s^2}, then find the understeer gradient in units of deg\si{/\g}. Is Niki understeering,
oversteering, or neutral steering?

Use the tire parameters \verb!f_tire.Ca_lin! and \verb!r_tire.Ca_lin!
for the front and rear cornering stiffnesses, respectively.

You are to modify the understeer gradient outputs \verb!K_radpmps2! and \verb!K_degpg! in the function template in \GRno{}.
Additionally, uncomment your choice of understeering, oversteering, or neutral steering.
