\secPr{Phase Portraits}

In this problem, we will look at phase portraits of Marty generated using PPLANE, a MATLAB plotting tool for phase plane analysis. There are code and instructions zipped up on Canvas. You will run the script called \verb|pplane2017.m|. You can either run PPLANE locally or in MATLAB Online.

Parameters you will need for Marty are given in the table below:

\begin{table}[h]
    \centering
    \begin{tabular}{c|c|c|c}
        Parameter & Value & Units & Meaning \\
        \hline
        $m$ & 1450 & kg & Mass\\
        $C_{\alpha f}$ & 60,000 & N/rad & Front cornering stiffness (lumped)\\
        $C_{\alpha r}$ & 160,000 & N/rad & Rear cornering stiffness (lumped)\\
        $\mu$ & 1.1 && Front/rear tire-road friction coefficient\\
        $L$ & 2.4 & m & Wheelbase\\
        $I_z$ & 2300 & kg m$^2$ & Yaw inertia\\
        $W_f$ & 0.33 & & Static front axle weight fraction
    \end{tabular}
    \caption{Marty Parameters}
    \label{tab:marty}
\end{table}
